To control the characters movement you may either use the wasd keys or the arrow keys. 

To walk forward you must press either up or w. While pressing forward you may press shift to run. 

To walk backwards you must press the down arrow key or s. 

While walking forward, backwards, running, or idling you may press the left and right arrow keys or a 
and d to turn your character. 

To jump, you must first be sprinting and you must press the space bar.
